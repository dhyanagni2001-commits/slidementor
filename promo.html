How it works

Open a slide.
Understand it instantly.

01

Install & open your PDF

Add SlideMentor to Chrome. Navigate to any lecture PDF — on Brightspace, Google Drive, or a local file. The extension auto-detects it and indexes the full document.

02

Click any page to explain

A visual grid shows all pages — gold for text-heavy, dim for image slides, blue ƒ for math slides. Click one and get a deep explanation in seconds.

03

Chat, ask, go deeper

Switch to Chat and ask anything about the slide. RAG retrieval pulls relevant context from your entire PDF to give precise, grounded answers.

04

Generate flashcards & study

One click generates 6-8 deep-understanding flashcards from any slide. Tap to flip, review before your exam — no extra app needed.
